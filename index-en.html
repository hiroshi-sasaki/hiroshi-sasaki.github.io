日本語

I run CARAS group at Tokyo Tech where we study computer architecture and computer security. Specifically, we work on improving the security of IoT devices and systems by enhancing computer architecture and system software. We also develop reliable and energy-efficient computing systmes, and perform workload and system characterization to understand emerging applications.

Before joining Tokyo Tech, I was at Columbia University. Before that I was a visiting research scientist at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center. I also did my postdoc at Kyushu University and The University of Tokyo. Even before that, I earned my PhD at The University of Tokyo.

I am looking for motivated PhD, MS, and undergrad students to join my group! If you are a Tokyo Tech student interested in working on cutting-edge research in computer architecture, computer security and computer systems in general send me an email with your CV and we can set up a meeting.
